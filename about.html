Meet Attia Altaf

Integrative Psychotherapist

I am Attia Altaf, a dedicated Integrative Psychotherapist with over 8 years of experience in helping individuals navigate the complexities of mental health in Pakistan.

My journey began when I noticed the huge gap in accessible mental health care in our country. Social stigma, traffic, and high costs often prevent people from seeking help.

"Healing with Attia" was born out of a desire to create a safe, non-judgmental digital space where you can share your burden without fear. Whether it's societal pressure, academic stress, or marital issues, I am here to listen.

Academic & Professional Qualifications

  • PhD in Media and Communication Sciences
  • Level 3 & 4 Diploma in Integrative Psychotherapy Counselling (CPCAB-UK)
  • Level 5 Diploma (In Progress)

Specialized Training

  • Hara Therapy (Inner Child, Shame, Guilt, Low Self Esteem)
  • Trauma Management
  • CBT - Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • DBT - Dialectical Behavior Therapy

Areas of Practice

  • Marriage Counseling — communication, joint family dynamics, trust repair, pre-marital counseling
  • Anxiety, Panic Attacks & Stress
  • Depression & Low Mood
  • OCD & Intrusive Thoughts (including Waswasa)
  • Trauma & PTSD
  • Burnout & Career Stress
